Unrivaled Location

Set in one of the best central locations in Dubai, Central Park is an ideal place for singles, couples and families to reside in. The area is easily connected with significant parts of Dubai as it is located between Al Wasl Road and the Sheikh Zayed Road. Central Park is considered a residential development where both urban spirit and closeness to nature will combine in the form of12 residential buildings and 20 different community amenities that will eventually be housed here.

Viridian and Celadon are the latest additions to the Central Park project. Viridian is a building with more than 100 residential units and offers 1 and 2-bedroom units with a living area from 670 sq.ft to 1,400 sq.ft. Celadon consists of three buildings with more than 150 units with total areas varying from 710 sq.ft to 2,170 sq.ft. All units are provided with green balconies, some of which will overlook the magnificent Central Park area.
